About us

Grupo VRS is a software development firm that builds advanced solutions for the financial services, healthcare, hospitality and media & entertainment industries. We believe teamwork, skills, commitment, and dedication are necessary to obtain excellent results. Our team stays up to date with the latest tools and frameworks in order to provide a wider range of options to our clients. Combining domain knowledge with offshore cost advantages and resource flexibility, Grupo VRS help clients optimize time-to-market and minimize software development risks in mission-critical systems.

What we do

Consulting & Engineering

When your team needs a hand we offer consulting services to help you make the right decisions for your project. Many companies have the personnel for development but not the experience in some languages or frameworks.

Dedicated team for your project

In recent years dedicated team service is becoming the norm. Working on a single project, engineers has the possibility to learn the client’s requirements thoroughly and complete them as efficiently as possible.

Software Development

Grupo VRS offers services for all phases of the software development life cycle. We work closely with our customers to define project requirements, develop and test the application, as well as write documentation and user’s manuals. We implement applications for a wide range of business needs, including Cloud Services.

Brand and UI/UX Design

Design is vital to the success of most products and services. The user interface and user experience (UI/UX) have an impact on sales, service cost, productive use, and almost every other aspect of business. We are here to help you design your product and services to maximize end-user satisfaction and increase profitability.

Mobile Apps

We can help you create a great looking custom mobile application that fits your needs perfectly. At Grupo VRS we use the latest tools and frameworks to deliver mobile apps for enterprise, entertainment, and social media. We are committed to helping you make your ideas become reality.

Desktop Apps

Many years of successful work in the area of custom application development has given us great experience. Now as an offshore professional software development company, we are able to implement projects of any degree of difficulty: from small business process automation up to large-scale enterprise management systems.

Outsourcing

Custom software development is our core business at Grupo VRS. Whether you are looking for consultants, designers, programmers or testers, we have great talent on board. We pride ourselves in being very responsive in customer service, strong on quality and effective at getting the job done.
